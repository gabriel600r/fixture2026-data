# Privacy Policy — Fixture Mundial 2026

**Last updated:** March 22, 2026

**Developer:** Gabriel Egea (EgeaINC)

## Introduction

Fixture Mundial 2026 ("the App") is a free mobile application for tracking the FIFA World Cup 2026 schedule, results, and predictions. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### Information stored locally on your device

The App stores the following data **exclusively on your device** using local storage (SharedPreferences):

- **User preferences:** theme (light/dark), language (Spanish/English).
- **Favorite teams:** the teams you mark as favorites.
- **Match results:** scores you enter manually.
- **Predictions (Prode):** your match predictions and points.
- **Notification settings:** whether match reminders are enabled.
- **Usage counters:** number of times the App has been opened (used solely to determine when to show the rating prompt).

This data **never leaves your device** and is **not transmitted** to any server, third party, or the developer.

### Network requests

The App makes the following network requests:

- **Results sync:** The App fetches match results from a publicly hosted JSON file on GitHub (`raw.githubusercontent.com`) to keep scores up to date. This is a read-only GET request. No personal data is sent in this request.
- **Google Fonts:** The App may download font files from Google Fonts servers on first use. Google's privacy policy applies to this request: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Information we do NOT collect

- We do **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number, address).
- We do **not** collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics data.
- We do **not** use tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
- We do **not** collect location data.
- We do **not** have user accounts or authentication.
- We do **not** share any data with third parties.

## Notifications

If you enable match notifications, the App schedules local notifications on your device using Android's built-in alarm system. This is handled entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

## In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases (e.g., "Buy me a coffee") processed through Google Play. These transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to [Google's Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/intl/en_us/about/play-terms/) and [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The developer does not collect or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect personal information from any user, it is safe for use by all ages.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by:

- Clearing the App's data from Android Settings > Apps > Fixture Mundial 2026 > Storage > Clear Data.
- Uninstalling the App.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
